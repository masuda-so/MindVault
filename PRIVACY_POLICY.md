# Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how MindVault handles your information.

## Data Collection

MindVault does not collect personal data from the app.

Your notes, tags, links, knowledge graph, embeddings, and app data are stored on your device by default. The developer does not operate a server for collecting or viewing this content.

## On-Device AI

AI features (organization, summaries, tags, related-note suggestions, and chat search) run on device using Apple Foundation Models on supported devices. Your note content is not sent to external AI services or developer-operated servers. Notes you mark as not AI-eligible are excluded from AI organization, embeddings, related-note candidates, and AI chat search.

## Import and Export

Markdown, JSON, and CSV import and export happen only when you choose them. Exported files are created on your device and are controlled by you.

## Purchases

Pro and Team subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. The developer does not receive your payment card information.

## Analytics and Advertising

MindVault does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking.
