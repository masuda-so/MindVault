## Privacy Policy

See [PRIVACY_POLICY.md](./PRIVACY_POLICY.md).

## Before Contacting Support

Please include:

- App version
- iOS version
- Device model
- A short description of what you expected to happen
- A short description of what happened instead

Do not include private note content, attachments, passwords, or payment details in support messages.

## Subscriptions

Pro and Team subscriptions are handled by Apple through the App Store. Purchases can be restored from the subscription screen in the app. The developer cannot view or manage your Apple ID payment method.
