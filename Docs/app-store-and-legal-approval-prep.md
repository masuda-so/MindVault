# App Store and Legal Approval Preparation

> This is an operational filing-preparation document, not legal advice.
> Do not submit a regulatory filing from an automated agent without representative approval and final professional review.


## Current Filing Position

MindVault is a local-first note application. No government application is currently identified for local note storage and on-device AI. Paid App Store release and future cloud/team features require platform approvals and legal documents.

## Required Before Paid Public Release

- App Store app review.
- StoreKit product/subscription approval if paid plans are enabled.
- Privacy Policy.
- Terms of Use or Apple standard EULA path.
- Support URL.
- License or all-rights-reserved clarification for the public repository.
- App Store privacy label mapping.

## Cloud / Team Feature Gate

Before enabling cloud sync, shared vaults, team administration, or server-side AI:

- Re-open privacy review.
- Define data processor/subprocessor list.
- Define admin access boundaries.
- Define deletion/export/account closure behavior.
- Update Privacy Policy and Terms.
